Math Revision for Under Tens

  • You will have 15 questions to answer and 30 seconds allowed for each one.
  • If you answer correctly within one second you will gain 30 points.
  • If you take five seconds to answer you will get only 25 points and so on.
  • If you answer incorrectly you get 0 points and move on to the next question.
  • For each question you will be asked to add, subtract, multiply (times) or divide.
  • You will see four options and one of them will be the correct answer.
  • ⚠️ Don't be fooled though, as some of the answers will be misleading.
  • For example, when asked What is 5 × 4?" you may see these options:
❌ wrong because it is
5 + 4, not 5 × 4		 ❌ wrong because it is
5 — 4, not 5 × 4
✅ correct because it is
5 × 4		 ❌ wrong because it is
5 × 9, not 5 × 4

Click the Start button above to start the game.

Good luck!
